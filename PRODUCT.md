# Product

## Register

product

## Users

OracleRead is for Android manga readers who manage libraries, install third-party extensions, browse sources, and read long-form chapters on phones and tablets. Users are often in a repeat-use workflow: opening the library, finding a title, resuming reading, organizing folders, or discovering sources.

## Product Purpose

OracleRead is a community-driven Mihon fork focused on a clean, stable, and modern manga reading experience. Success means preserving Mihon compatibility and reliability while giving OracleRead a clear visual identity, proper attribution, and polished reader/library workflows.

## Brand Personality

Calm, atmospheric, practical. The app should feel modern and lightly expressive without compromising reading speed, accessibility, or familiarity.

## Anti-references

Do not hide the project lineage. Avoid marketing-style screens inside core workflows, cluttered controls, loud animation that distracts from reading, and changes that make inherited Mihon/Tachiyomi behavior harder to recognize.

## Design Principles

- Reading comes first: atmosphere should stay quiet behind content.
- Familiar workflows, clearer labels: improve organization and controls without surprising existing users.
- Lineage stays visible: Tachiyomi, Mihon, and OracleRead should be understandable in the app and docs.
- Fast access beats deep settings for common states like incognito.
- Motion is ambient, optional-feeling, and respectful of reduced motion.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use accessible contrast, keep motion subtle, and provide reduced-motion alternatives for ambient animation. Icon-only actions need meaningful content descriptions. Color choices must not be the sole indicator for folder identity.
